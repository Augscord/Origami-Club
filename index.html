The Art of the Fold

The ancient art of Origami is an important piece of Japanese culture that has become popular in all parts of the world. The Art of carefuly folding pieces of paper into uinique and intricate shapes of objects or animals is a long but relaxing process that many people have taken up. But there are some people who do not know where to begin or even know how to create origami themselves. That is where we come in! We are a small group of origami artists and enthusiasts that are dedicated to teach you the beautiful craft of origami folding. This is a perfect place to learn the art of folding as well as traditional japanese cultures in a calm and relaxing environment.
